Convert VMDK from Thin to Eager Zero

This is one way to convert a thin provisioned VMDK into a thick provisioned eager zeroed VMDK. I was unable to mount a thin provisioned VMDK in read/write mode using vmware-mount.exe, so this is how I worked around it. You will need the VMware Virtual Disk Development Kit (VDDK) to do this.

  • After installing VDDK, open cmd.exe and use vmware-vdiskmanager to convert the VMDK.
cd c:\Program Files (x86)\VMware\VMware Virtual Disk Development Kit\bin
vmware-vdiskmanager -r c:\thin_provisioned.vmdk -t 2 c:\thick_eager_zero.vmdk
